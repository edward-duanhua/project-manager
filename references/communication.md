# Communication & Reporting (沟通与汇报)

This module outlines the strategies and processes for effective project communication, ensuring stakeholders are informed and engaged.

## Core Concepts

*   **Stakeholder Analysis**: Understanding the needs, interests, and power of project stakeholders.
*   **Communication Planning**: Determining who needs what information, when, and how.
*   **Information Distribution**: Sending relevant information to stakeholders in a timely manner.
*   **Performance Reporting**: Collecting and distributing performance information (status reports, progress measurements, forecasts).

## Workflow

1.  **Identify Stakeholders**: Determine all people or organizations impacted by the project.
    *   *Tools: Stakeholder Register.*
2.  **Analyze Needs**: Understand what information each stakeholder needs and their preferred communication method.
    *   *Example: "Sponsor needs weekly summary; Team needs daily standup."*
3.  **Plan Communications**: Define the communication approach.
    *   *Format: Email, Meeting, Dashboard.*
    *   *Frequency: Daily, Weekly, Monthly.*
    *   *Responsible: Who sends it.*
4.  **Distribute Information**: Make relevant information available to project stakeholders as planned.
    *   *Tools: Project Management Information System (PMIS), Email, Intranet.*
5.  **Manage Expectations**: Communicate and work with stakeholders to meet their needs and address issues as they occur.
6.  **Report Performance**: Collect and distribute performance information, including status reports, progress measurements, and forecasts.

## Templates

### Communication Matrix

1.  **Stakeholder**: Name/Role.
2.  **Information Needs**: What do they need to know?
3.  **Format**: Email/Meeting/Report.
4.  **Frequency**: Daily/Weekly/Ad-hoc.
5.  **Owner**: Who is responsible.

### Weekly Status Report Structure

1.  **Executive Summary**: High-level status (RAG).
2.  **Accomplishments**: Key achievements this week.
3.  **Planned Activities**: Goals for next week.
4.  **Risks & Issues**: Current blockers and mitigations.
5.  **Metrics**: Schedule variance, budget variance.
